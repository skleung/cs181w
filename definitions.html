Definitions

What is Vigilantism?

Vigilantism is the act of taking the law into one’s own hands. Vigilante behavior typically involves varying levels of unlawful actions to effect justice according to an individual’s or a group’s understanding of right and wrong.

What is Digilantism?

Digital vigilantism is the phenomenon of vigilant acts taken through the Internet or carried out through applications that depend on the internet. Essentially, citizens who decide to undertake law enforcement through the Internet without legal authority.
